Archeological Sites

Get to visit wonderful places likeThe Great Pyramid of Giza is not only the most recognized of Egypt’s pyramids, it’s also the only of the Seven wonders of the ancient World that’s still standing. The Great Pyramid is impressive for many reasons, but one of the most interesting characteristics of this celebrated monument is that it can also tell time. The structure actually acts as an enormous sundial, with its shadow telling the hour by falling on marks made in the stone.
